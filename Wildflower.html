Alaska Wildflower Identifier(In Progress)

By Luke Pratt

I created this project because my grandmother Verna Pratt created an award winning(*) book called "Field Guide to Alaskan Wildflowers"(*). This made me interested of carrying on that idea and creating an app to do the "job" of the book and help people identify what alaskan wildflower they are looking at.

Customers: People in Alaska

Description: Application where users submit a picture of a flower and using AI, Machine learning, and Computer vision the application tells you what flower is in the picture and some details about it.
