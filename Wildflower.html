Alaska Wildflower Classifier

By Luke Pratt

About: I created this project because my grandmother Verna Pratt created an award winning(*) book called "Field Guide to Alaskan Wildflowers"(*). This made me interested of carrying on that idea and creating an app to do the "job" of the book and help people identify what flowers they are looking at. I am still working on expanding the dataset used in this project.
